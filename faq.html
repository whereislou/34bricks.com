FAQs

Where the hell is Korweinguboora?

Great question. Koreweinguboora is around 10 minutes south of Dayelsford, and is a local indigenous word meaning "place of many springs". Given there is a natural spring that runs under the property, and water falls generously from the sky on an almost constant basis, this is no real surprise.

What the the chickens' names?

In an (failed) effort to remain detached from the chickens (in case of foxes and other horrific natural disasters in which they may come in contact), we named all of our chickens after the lovely person who bought them for us: Nick Haslett. Thanks Nick for our chickens, Nick, Nick, Nick, Nick and Nick.

The only exceptions is Piglet, a gift from our friend Jen, who was initially convinced Piglet must be a rooster (due to her impressive girth) but instead just turned out to be the loveliest and stupidest of them all. You can pick her because she's a foot taller than the rest.
