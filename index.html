We spent years dreaming about living the simply life, cooking our own food, growing our own produce and enjoying the company of chickens and sheep. Enter 34Bricks: our home in the country, our peaceful escape from city life. Join us on our travels of learning how to grow vegetables, to build pizza ovens & charcoal spits, to cure smallgoods and pickle the plentiful produce that grows on our property. Meet our beautiful chickens, rejoice with us when we succeed, share our disappointment when we do not, and cook with us along the way.
